Where is Haspa based and what is its mission?

Based in Kerala, India, Haspa International Traders Pvt. Ltd. exports authentic Indian products to global markets with a core philosophy: the "2H Policy" — Healthy and Happy. We aim to deliver wellness-focused, sustainable products and build trust-driven international trade relationships.

What makes Haspa stand out in the market?

We are registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs and certified by APEDA and FSSAI. Haspa offers premium quality products at balanced pricing — neither too cheap nor too expensive — to meet international standards without compromising trust and safety.

What types of products does Haspa export?

Haspa exports a diverse range: spices and extracts, food items (rice, pulses, flours, sauces), ready-to-eat and ready-to-cook meals, fruits and vegetables (fresh & frozen), edible and essential oils, herbal goods, coir products, garments, handicrafts, coconuts, kitchen tools, and eco-friendly packaging.

Do you focus on sustainable packaging?

Yes. In alignment with our sustainability mission, we provide eco-conscious packaging alternatives that reduce environmental impact.

What services does Haspa offer to clients?

Our key services include product traceability, quality control, private labeling, custom packaging, and demand-based sourcing. We also specialize in global logistics and personalized customer support.

Where does Haspa export to?

We serve clients across Europe and the Middle East — including Germany, Netherlands, France, Italy, Czech Republic, UAE, and Saudi Arabia.
